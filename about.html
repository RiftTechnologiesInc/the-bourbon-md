About Us

The Beginning

A Place Worth Savoring

At The Bourbon MD, we bring together the timeless pairing of spirits and cigars. Our selections are crafted for those who appreciate depth, tradition, and a place to unwind with good company and great flavor.

Nestled in the heart of Bluffton, Indiana, we created a space where the pace slows down and quality takes center stage. Whether you're a seasoned bourbon connoisseur or discovering the world of premium cigars for the first time, you'll find a welcoming atmosphere and a knowledgeable team ready to guide you.

Every selection on our menu has been carefully curated — from rare single-barrel bourbons to hand-rolled cigars from the world's finest producers. We believe the best moments happen when you take your time and enjoy the finer things in life.
