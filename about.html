The Founders

Joey Mossburg & Nathan Dirig

Bourbon & Cigar Connoisseurs

With a deep appreciation for great bourbon and fine cigars, the founders of The Bourbon MD have spent years exploring distilleries and cigar lounges. Their passion for the craft and the culture surrounding it inspired them to create a space where others can discover, connect, and enjoy the experience together.

Our Story

Our History

1800's

The Wiecking Cigar Factory

Originally established in the late 1800s, the Wiecking family cigar factory once produced over 15,000 hand-rolled cigars each week using Bluffton-grown tobacco. To honor the rich history of the building, we created the Wiecking VIP Room — a private space where select members can relax and enjoy an exclusive experience.

2024

Renovations

Renovations on the historic building at 209 W. Market Street began in 2024 with a vision to create a one-of-a-kind bourbon and cigar lounge. As the space took shape, the rich history of the Wiecking cigar factory was uncovered, adding an unexpected layer of legacy to the project. What started as a fresh new chapter quickly became a tribute to Bluffton's past, blending old and new into a unique destination.

2025

A New Tradition Begins

By 2025, The Bourbon MD officially opened its doors, welcoming guests to experience a new standard of bourbon, cigars, and community. With a focus on premium selections, curated events, and an atmosphere rooted in both craftsmanship and hospitality, The Bourbon MD continues to build on the rich legacy of the space — creating a place where history, quality, and connection come together.
